Tips Of Flower World!

According to the morphological characteristics of flowers, flowers are usually divided into two categories: herbaceous flowers and woody flowers. Herbaceous flowers have soft stems and woody flowers have hard stems.

According to the growth habits and morphological characteristics of flowers, they can be divided into herbaceous flowers, woody flowers, succulent flowers and aquatic flowers.

According to the use, flowers can be divided into cut flowers (such as carnation, Calla Lily, etc.), indoor flowers (such as Clivia, etc.), garden flowers (such as rose, etc.), medicinal flowers (such as peony, etc.), spice flowers (such as brandy, etc.) and edible flowers (lily, Flammulina).

Floral Language

Rose

Rose is known as the flower of love, its flower language is beautiful love, courage, love and beauty. Moreover, different colors of roses have different meanings. The meaning of red rose is hot love, which is usually used to express passionate love. It is a bouquet to express love on Valentine's day, anniversary, marriage proposal and other occasions.

Paeonia suffruticosa

The flower language of Paeonia suffruticosa is "rich and auspicious" and "popular". It has unique pattern, changeable color, very beautiful and popular, so it can also represent those who are popular. Its moral is "happy life", "happy life" and "family wealth". Giving it to others can express the good wishes of wishing each other happiness and happiness forever.

Eustoma grandiflorum

The flower language of Platycodon grandiflorum is eternal love, which is related to love and implies constant love and eternal feelings. It also has a flower language is no regret, that no regret feelings. Different colors of Platycodon grandiflorum have different flower language.
